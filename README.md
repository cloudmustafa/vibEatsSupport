# vibEatsSupport# VibEats

Discover your next favorite restaurant, café, bar, bakery, or hidden food gem with VibEats.

VibEats helps food lovers explore nearby places, save favorites, rate their experiences with the exclusive BiteMeter™, and share beautiful review cards with friends on social media.

## Features

- Discover nearby restaurants and cafés
- Apple Maps integration
- Search by cuisine or restaurant
- Personalized recommendations
- Save favorite places
- BiteMeter™ ratings
- Restaurant reviews
- Share to Instagram, Facebook, Messages, and more
- Beautiful social cards
- Modern, privacy-first design
